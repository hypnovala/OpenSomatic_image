Voiceover (20–35 seconds)

Delivery notes:
• calm tone
• slow pacing
• breath pauses

Script:
If you’re home after a hospital shift and you still feel… buzzy? On edge?

That’s your nervous system staying in high alert. Let’s help it downshift in seven minutes.

First—pause at the doorway. Inhale gently… and make your exhale longer. Do that three times.

Next—legs up the wall, or on the couch. One hand on your belly. Let the exhale soften your ribs.

Now—hum softly on the exhale. Just “mmm.” Five slow breaths.

Last—orient. Slowly look around and find five neutral objects. Let your eyes land, and soften.

Then choose one “done” cue—shower, tea, or changing clothes—to tell your body: we’re safe.

Education only, not medical advice.
