Delivery notes
• calm tone
• slow pacing
• breath pauses

Voiceover (20–35 seconds)
If your shift is over but your body still feels on-call… this is for you.

Take a moment to orient. Slowly look left… and right… and name five neutral things you see.

Now do two physiological sighs: inhale through your nose… little top-up sip… and a long, slow exhale.
[pause]
Again.

Shake out your hands… your arms… your legs… just enough to let the day move through.
[pause]
And then stillness.

As you change shoes or wash your hands, say: “Shift is over. I’m off-duty.”

Education only, not medical advice.
