Voiceover (20–35 seconds)

Delivery notes:
• calm tone
• slow pacing
• breath pauses

Script:
Nurses—before you drive home, do this three-minute nervous system reset.

Because you clocked out… but your body might still be in high alert.

First: three physiological sighs.
Inhale… top it off with a second short inhale…
then a long, slow exhale.
Do that three times.

Next: unclench scan.
Soften your jaw… relax your tongue… drop your shoulders… unclench your hands.

Now: butterfly taps.
Cross your arms and do gentle alternating taps while you breathe out slowly.

Last: orient.
Name three colors… two shapes… and one sound you can hear.

That’s your signal: the emergency is over.

Education only, not medical advice.
